Who is Nokotan?

Nokotan is a character from the anime "My Deer Friend Nokotan." The first episode aired July 3, 2024 and is produced by Wit Studio.

Picture of Nokotan riding a deer. Picture is taken from This Forbes article

One of these characters are Nokotan (Noko Shikanoko), my personal favorite! She is an... Odd character, we could say the least. Some of her quirks are things like:

  • Obsession with deer crackers
  • Constant happiness (Although, she can show emotions like anger and sadness)
  • Likes shortening names (Shortening Torako Koshi's name to "Koshitan" and her own name to "Nokotan")

Nokotan's obsession with deer crackers borders on broderline insane. She goes to such great lengths to where in something like (For example) Season 1 Episode 2, Nokotan sees a deer cracker, but it's very obviously.

A screenshot from the anime intro where it shows Nokotan with a pile of deer crackers. Image is taken from Screen rants website

Another thing that should be known about Nokotan is that she's extremely naive and childlike. If you tell her something, there's a high chance she'll belive you. This makes sense context wise due to it being a comedy anime, and it really doesn't take itself seriously most of the time.

Even though it makes absolutely no sense that a deer/human hybrid that can basically bend the laws of physics and life to exist, most of the characters in the series don't mind.
